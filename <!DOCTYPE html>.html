UWEC CSSA Website – Design Explanation

This document explains how the proposed UWEC CSSA website design meets the expectations and goals of the project. The focus of this design is clarity, accessibility, and usefulness for students.

1. Website Purpose

The main purpose of the UWEC CSSA website is to serve as an information and communication platform for Chinese students and international students at the University of Wisconsin–Eau Claire. The website is designed to introduce the organization, promote events, and provide helpful resources for new and current students.

2. Target Users

  • Current Chinese students at UWEC
  • Incoming international students
  • Non-Chinese students interested in CSSA events
  • University staff and other student organizations

3. Key Website Features

Home Page

The Home page provides a clear introduction to CSSA. It includes the organization name, a brief description, and visual highlights from past events. This helps users quickly understand what CSSA is and what it offers.

About Us Page

The About Us page explains the mission and goals of CSSA. This page builds credibility and helps users understand the purpose of the organization and its role on campus.

Events Page

The Events page lists upcoming and past activities organized by CSSA. This feature encourages student participation and keeps members informed about social and cultural events.

New Students Guide

The New Students Guide provides practical information for incoming students, such as campus life tips and local resources. This feature adds real value and supports students during their transition to UWEC.

Our Team Page

The Our Team page introduces the CSSA executive board members. By showing names and roles, the website appears more organized and trustworthy.

Contact and Join Us Page

This page allows users to contact CSSA and learn how to join. It includes email information, social media links, and a sign-up form. This supports communication and member recruitment.

4. How the Design Meets Expectations

The website design meets the expectations by focusing on clear structure, simple navigation, and user-friendly content. Each page has a specific purpose, and the overall layout ensures that users can easily find the information they need.

The design also reflects agreement among group members on the main features of the website, such as event promotion and student support. This HTML page demonstrates my understanding of the website goals and the agreed-upon features discussed within the group.
