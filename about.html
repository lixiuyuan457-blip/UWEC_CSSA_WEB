Our History

The UWEC Chinese Student & Scholar Association was founded to create a supportive community for Chinese students on campus. Over the years, we have grown from a small group to a vibrant organization with hundreds of active members.

Today, CSSA plays an important role in helping students adjust to life in the United States, celebrating Chinese culture, and building friendships across cultural boundaries.
